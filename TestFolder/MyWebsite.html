Welcome to this website about Heidegger, and his famous book, Being and Time.

To start, here are some fun facts about Heidegger as a person

  • Heidegger calls the human being Dasein

You can find his text in the public domain here.

The text begins by discussing the idea that being is time and that we are not sperate from it, nor are we seperate from the objects in the world.

He says that we should live as being-towards-death. He says that the most important part of our lives is our death and our finitude.
